## OPUS parallel corpora

- Project: OPUS — https://opus.nlpl.eu/
- Usage: Chinese–Vietnamese aligned sentence pairs used to select one complete example sentence per HSK word.
- Corpora used: Tatoeba, Asian Language Treebank (ALT), TED2020, WikiMatrix and OpenSubtitles.

The individual corpora retain their respective terms:

- Tatoeba: CC BY 2.0 FR.
- ALT: CC BY 4.0; translations copyright NICT.
- WikiMatrix: CC BY-SA 4.0.
- TED2020: TED Talks Usage Policy and the terms of its original sources.
- OpenSubtitles: terms of the original subtitle sources as distributed by OPUS.

Attribution reference: Jörg Tiedemann, “Parallel Data, Tools and Interfaces,” LREC 2012. Sentence source identifiers are retained in each vocabulary record.
